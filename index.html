CORDOVA MUSEUM

Like most of the towns in the Province of Cebu, Cordova also got its name after a place in Spain. It was Governor General Rafael Echague who created this town (composed of the barrios of Gabi, Day-as, and Pilipul) on May 22, 1863. He chose the name Cordova which means "stark nakedness and bare". Spain, Mexico and Argentina and the six states of the United States of America - Alaska, South Carolina, Illinois, Alabama, New Mexico, & Tennessee have cities also named Cordova.

CORDOVA REF VILLAGE RESORT

Parkside Cordova Reef Village Resort is located at Cordova on Mactan Island, around 40 minutes from Cebu City. This beachfront resort is in the coastal town of Cordova on Mactan Island and is surrounded by the Camotes sea to the east and Bohol Strait to the south. The resort has a 808 square-meter freeform swimming pool and a health club and spa. Villa design at Parkside Cordiva Reef Village Resort is inspired by African tribal villages and guests are welcome to their villas with a fruit basket.

PAROLA SEA VIEW RESTAURANT

Enjoy authentic Filipino food while gazing at the ocean in an open, hidden restaurant in Cordova District of Mactan Island. Immerse yourself in the beautiful ambiance of the ocean and the dazzling sun views.

RORO PORT

This is a wide road from the port of Cordova going to the main highway of the town. Most people visiting this plays to unwind and relax. Some were group of friends and families did their jogs and some did zumbas. Many bikers also love to visit here. After their long bikes they can stop the place and rest for a while together with their bike mates. If they are hungry, there are also food vendors near the area.

LANTAW FLOATING RESTAURANT

Lantaw is a Bisaya word that translates to “look out”. It is a floating restaurant located in Day-as, Cordova where you can enjoy having an intimate dinner with your family while watching the sun set in the horizon with the calming sound of seawater surrounding the restaurant. Aside from the view, Lantaw Floating Native Restaurant is known for their palatable dishes that are sure to make you crave for a second serving.
